# Atlas

**Your AI Operating System**

Atlas is a powerful, self-improving AI platform designed to run real work for you. It combines long-running agents (Aether), intelligent workflow learning, a strong governance layer (Judge), and deep tool use to act as a true operating system for individuals and teams.

Instead of just chatting with an AI, Atlas **does the work** — reliably, observably, and under your control.

### ✨ Core Features

- **Aether** — Your long-running, goal-driven AI agents. Give it a mission and it breaks it down, executes it, and delivers real results.
- **Workflow Learning** — Atlas watches how you work and automatically discovers, models, and suggests recurring workflows.
- **Judge System** — A governance layer that reviews high-risk actions before they happen.
- **Skills System** — Modular, reusable capabilities that can be discovered, installed, and upgraded.
- **Connectivity Hub** — Secure connections to Gmail, Drive, Slack, browsers, and other tools.
- **Immutable History Ledger** — Full audit trail of every action, decision, and change.
- **Private LLM Support** — Run Atlas with your own local or private models for sensitive data.

### 🚀 Who is Atlas for?

- **Individuals & Power Users** — Run your personal operating system locally or in the cloud.
- **Teams & Small Businesses** — Automate repetitive work across departments.
- **Enterprises** — Deploy Atlas with strict governance, private models, and commercial support.
